WHATS NEW?
  • New blocks: Minecraft 1.21 introduces three new blocks, including the Crafter for automatic crafting, Trial Spawner for spawning enemies, and Copper Bulb for lighting.

  • Trial Chamber: Explore the Trial Chamber to encounter the Trial Spawner, facing tough challenges for loot rewards or risking gear loss upon defeat.
